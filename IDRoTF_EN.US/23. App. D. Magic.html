Two books are described in this section. Characters can find @item[The Codicil of White|IDRotF] in Auril's abode (see @UUID[JournalEntry.bGFHJOs6yQKK0dQn.JournalEntryPage.0000000000000015]{chapter 5}) and @item[The Incantations of Iriolarthas|IDRotF] in the Netherese necropolis hidden under the Reghed Glacier (see @UUID[JournalEntry.bGFHJOs6yQKK0dQn.JournalEntryPage.0000000000000017]{chapter 7}).
